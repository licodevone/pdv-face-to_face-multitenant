Tinted, fully-rounded pill for stock and cash states.

```jsx
<Badge tone="success" dot>Disponível</Badge>
<Badge tone="warn" dot>Estoque baixo</Badge>
<Badge tone="danger" dot>Esgotado</Badge>
<Badge tone="neutral">Caixa aberto</Badge>
```

Tones: `success` / `warn` / `danger` / `neutral`. Uppercase text is applied by the class.
